# Sample data/legal_faqs.txt
What is a will?
A will is a legal declaration of the intention of a person regarding the distribution of their property after death.

How can I evict a tenant in India?
Eviction requires a legal notice under rent laws and a court order. Illegal eviction is punishable.

What happens if someone dies without a will?
Their property is distributed as per succession laws, like the Hindu Succession Act for Hindus.

Can elderly parents seek protection from abusive children?
Yes, under the Maintenance and Welfare of Parents and Senior Citizens Act, 2007, they can file complaints with SDM.

Is registration of will mandatory in India?
No, but it is advisable for authenticity.
